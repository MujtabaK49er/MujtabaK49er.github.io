Website Evaluations

Good Website

The Apple website showcases a sleek, minimalist design that enhances user experience and brand image.

  • Contrast: High-quality images and bold headlines provide clear contrast and draw attention.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, layout, and colors strengthens brand identity across all pages.
  • Alignment: Elements are perfectly aligned using a grid-based structure that ensures balance.
  • Proximity: Related information is grouped logically, making it easy to find and digest content.

Bad Website

The Yale School of Art website has a chaotic layout that hinders navigation and readability.

  • Contrast: Low contrast between text and background often makes content hard to read.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent styles and typography create a disjointed visual experience.
  • Alignment: The lack of alignment creates a disorganized appearance and confuses users.
  • Proximity: Poor grouping of related items makes it difficult to navigate and understand content flow.

Overall, the website neglects key CRAP principles, resulting in a cluttered and frustrating user interface.
